Play Style Helper

                    
Techniques to Make It Sound Great

                

                

                    Use these techniques to add personality and feeling to your playing!
                

            

            

                

                    
🎸 Guitar Techniques

                    

                        / = Slide Up

                        \ = Slide Down

                        h = Hammer-On

                        p = Pull-Off

                        PM = Palm Mute

                        v = Vibrato

                        x = Muted/Slap
                    

                

                

                    
🎹 Piano Techniques

                    

                        Legato = Smooth, connected notes

                        Staccato = Short, detached notes

                        Chords = Play notes together

                        Arpeggios = Play notes one after another
                    

                

                

                    
🎵 General Tips

                    

                        Rhythm = Keep a steady beat

                        Dynamics = Play soft and loud

                        Space = Leave gaps between notes

                        Feeling = Play with emotion!
